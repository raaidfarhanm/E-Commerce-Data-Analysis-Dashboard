Amazon Product Analytics Dashboard

Overview
This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard built using Amazon product data. The analysis focuses on product performance, customer ratings, review trends, pricing strategies, and discount patterns to generate actionable business insights.
The dashboard is designed to help stakeholders understand customer preferences, identify high-performing products, evaluate pricing effectiveness, and support data-driven decision-making.

Business Insights

  •	Most products maintain ratings above 4 stars. 

  •	Product popularity is strongly influenced by customer reviews. 

  •	High discounts do not necessarily lead to higher ratings. 

  •	Significant pricing variations exist across product categories. 

  •	Customer engagement patterns help identify top-performing products.
